Supported Data Format

The Pitcher Profiler works with standard Statcast CSV files containing pitch-by-pitch data. Required columns include: player_name, pitch_name, release_speed, pfx_x, pfx_z, plate_x, plate_z, description, events, and other standard Statcast fields.
